Peruvian cuisine includes a whole cookbook of dishes full of flavor and varied textures. Although many of its most popular recipes are made up of different types of meat, dishes based on pasta or fish also stand out. The famous Peruvian green noodles, also known as Peruvian green spaghetti, have their origin in the Ithalian migrants brought to that South American country. Currently, it is part of the dayli food of Peruvian and it is easy to understand why. They are delicious and cooking them is not complicated. Its flavor is very reminiscent of pesto pasta due to the presence of basil.
