The salted noodle is a typical dish of Peruvian cuisine, typical of chifa cuisine. The name of the dish comes from the technique known as "stir-frying", in which food is fried over high heat and in small pieces. To make this dish, a portion of cooked noodles, vegetables and portions of meat are sautéed to taste. The seasoning or dressing comes from Chinese spices and sesame oil.
